# Hackathon Rules

## 1) General Rules
- This is a **1-day in-person hackathon**.
- Teams must have **2–4 members** (solo only with organizer approval).
- Projects should target **social good outcomes**.

## 2) Build Rules
- Core implementation must be completed during hackathon hours.
- Prior learning, research, and setup are allowed before event day.
- Reusing open-source libraries is allowed with proper attribution.

## 3) AI Requirement
- Every project must meaningfully use **z.ai tools and/or APIs**.
- Teams should document where and how AI is used.

## 4) Ethics & Safety
- No harmful, abusive, discriminatory, or illegal use cases.
- Respect privacy; avoid exposing personal/sensitive data.

## 5) Submission Rules
- Submit before the official deadline.
- Late submissions may be penalized or disqualified.
- Submission must follow [submission.md](./submission.md).

## 6) Disqualification Conditions
- Plagiarism or copied final projects
- Fake demos / broken repos with no meaningful code
- Violation of [code-of-conduct.md](./code-of-conduct.md)

## 7) Decision Policy
Judging panel and organizer decisions are final.
