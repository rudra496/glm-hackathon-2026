# Code of Conduct

We are committed to an inclusive, respectful, and collaborative event.

## Expected Behavior
- Be respectful and constructive.
- Support beginners and encourage teamwork.
- Give credit to others' work.

## Unacceptable Behavior
- Harassment, hate speech, discrimination
- Cheating, plagiarism, sabotage
- Sharing private data without consent

## Enforcement
Organizers may warn, penalize, or disqualify participants for violations.

## Reporting
Report concerns privately to the organizer (Rudra Sarker) during the event.
