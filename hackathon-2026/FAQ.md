# Frequently Asked Questions

## Who can join?
University students (individuals or teams up to 4 members).

## Is this online only?
Yes. All stages are fully remote.

## Can first-year students join?
Yes.

## Can we change team members after starting?
Allowed only until Day 2, with organizer approval.

## Can we use open-source libraries?
Yes, with proper attribution.

## Can we use AI coding tools?
Yes. You must remain accountable for your code and mention major AI assistance in README.

## Is a prototype enough?
Yes, but it must be functional and demonstrable.

## Do we need to deploy the app?
No paid hosting required. Local/demo video proof is acceptable.

## What if our app is unfinished?
Submit what you have before deadline. Partially complete projects can still be evaluated.

## How will winners be announced?
Through Discord and official social posts.
