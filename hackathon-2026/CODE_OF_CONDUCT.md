# Code of Conduct

## Purpose

AI Builders Hackathon 2026 aims to provide a respectful, inclusive, and collaborative environment for all participants.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Welcome diverse perspectives and skill levels.
- Provide feedback professionally.
- Communicate clearly in official channels.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, hate speech, or personal attacks
- Spamming or disruptive behavior
- Sharing private information without consent
- Academic dishonesty or plagiarism

## Reporting

Report concerns to organizers via:
- Discord ticket/support channel
- Organizer email: `aibuildersbd.organizers@gmail.com`

## Enforcement

Organizers may issue warnings, remove messages, disqualify teams, or ban users for serious or repeated violations.
